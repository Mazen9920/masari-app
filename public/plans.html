Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Shopify app free?

Yes. The Revvo app on the Shopify App Store is completely free to install. To use the integration, you need an active Growth or Pro subscription on the Revvo mobile app.

How do I subscribe?

Download the Revvo app on iOS or Android, create an account, and subscribe from the app's settings. Payment is handled through the mobile app.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Revvo app. Your data remains accessible, and your Shopify connection stays active until the billing period ends.

What happens if my subscription expires?

Your Shopify sync will pause and your account will revert to the free Launch plan. Your data is preserved — resubscribe to resume syncing.
