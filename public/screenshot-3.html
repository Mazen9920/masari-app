Welcome to Revvo

Revvo syncs your Shopify orders and inventory to a mobile app where you can track COGS, calculate profit per order, and manage two-way inventory.

How to connect your store
1
Open the Revvo mobile app
Launch Revvo on your iOS or Android device and sign in to your account.
2
Connect your Shopify store
Go to Manage → Shopify Integration, tap Connect Store, enter your store domain, and authorize access.
3
Manage settings here
Once connected, return to this page to toggle sync settings, change your Shopify location, view sync history, or disconnect.
